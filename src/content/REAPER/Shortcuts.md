## Transport

```shortcuts
Play / Stop          | Space
Record               | Ctrl+R
Toggle repeat        | R
Go to start          | Home
Go to end            | End
```

## Editing

```shortcuts
Split item at cursor | S
Open action list     | Ctrl+Shift+P
Undo                 | Ctrl+Z
Redo                 | Ctrl+Shift+Z
Duplicate item       | Ctrl+D
Crossfade selected   | Ctrl+F
```

> [!tip] Press `Ctrl+Shift+P` to open the action list — it is the fastest way to find anything you have not memorized yet.

## Views

Toggle the mixer with `Ctrl+M`, and the media explorer with `Ctrl+Alt+X`.

> [!note] Defaults shown are for REAPER 7+ on Windows. On macOS, swap `Ctrl` for `⌘` in most bindings.
